About

Hi, I'm Maja Kryztel A. Adonis , the creator of this website. I am a first year Computer Engineering (CoE) student at National College Science and Technology. I've create this site to share my artworks, recognizing its potential for both well-being and professional opportunity.

I'm fascinated by the connection of technology and arts that creates a field for innovation and creativity. As an artist , I bring a strong foundation in visual communication and creative expression. However, I understand that the future of art and design is inextricably linked to technology. I'm dedicated to bridging the gap in my technological knowledge, eager to learn new tools and techniques that will enhance my artistic capabilities. By combining my artistic intuition with improved technological proficiency, I aim to contribute to projects that are not only aesthetically stunning but also technologically groundbreaking.
